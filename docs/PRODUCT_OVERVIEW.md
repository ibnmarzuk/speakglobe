# Product Overview

SpeakGlobal is an AI Communication Coach platform designed to empower individuals to become confident global communicators.

## Problem

Many people struggle with communication in professional settings, particularly when interacting internationally. This leads to missed opportunities, lack of confidence, and reduced productivity.

## Solution

SpeakGlobal provides a personalized AI coaching experience that helps users practice and improve their communication skills through real-time AI interactions, feedback, and progress tracking.

## Target Audience

- University Students
- Job Seekers
- Software Developers
- Remote Workers
- Freelancers
- Founders
- Professionals
- Creators

## Value Proposition

SpeakGlobal provides constructive, encouraging, and practical feedback to help users communicate clearly and confidently.

## Core Features

- AI-powered practice sessions
- Real-time voice interaction
- Context-aware coaching
- Personalized feedback
- Progress tracking

## Future Vision

To expand beyond voice coaching to include real-time video analysis, integration with major communication platforms (Slack, Zoom, Meet), and global community-driven learning.
