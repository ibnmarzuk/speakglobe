# Landing Page

The landing page is designed to guide users through the SpeakGlobal value proposition.

## Sections
- **Hero:** Compelling title and CTA for new and returning users.
- **Problem/Solution:** Why communication matters and how AI helps.
- **AI Preview:** Live AI conversation preview.
- **How It Works:** Steps to start practicing.
- **Practice Scenarios:** Interviews, meetings, etc.
- **Benefits:** Key advantages of SpeakGlobal.
- **Success Stories:** Testimonials from real users.
- **Pricing:** Clear plan options.
- **FAQ:** Common questions.
- **Footer:** Links and brand info.
