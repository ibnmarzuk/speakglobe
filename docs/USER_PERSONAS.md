# User Personas

## Job Seeker
- **Goals:** Get hired for a dream role.
- **Pain points:** Anxiety during interviews, lack of clarity in answers.
- **Motivation:** Career advancement.

## Software Developer
- **Goals:** Communicate technical concepts clearly to non-technical stakeholders.
- **Pain points:** Struggling to explain complex technical designs.
- **Motivation:** Better collaboration, career growth.

## Remote Worker
- **Goals:** Communicate effectively in asynchronous and synchronous remote environments.
- **Pain points:** Miscommunication due to text-only communication, feeling disconnected.
- **Motivation:** Building stronger relationships with the team.

## Founder
- **Goals:** Pitch effectively to investors and attract top talent.
- **Pain points:** Inability to articulate the product vision clearly and concisely.
- **Motivation:** Product success, funding.
